Strait Outta Hormuz

Dodge the chaos. Keep the oil.

How to Play

🚢 Drag your finger up and down to steer the tanker.

💣 Dodge mines, torpedoes, and drones.

💰 Oil value increases every second you survive.

💥 Hit anything and you lose everything.

🏆 Beat your high score. Share your best run.

Tips

Near misses earn bonus oil — the closer you get without dying, the more you earn. Chain multiple near misses for combo bonuses.

Drones track your position but they're slow. Keep moving vertically to stay ahead of them.

Watch for the red warning indicator on the right edge — that means a torpedo is incoming.
